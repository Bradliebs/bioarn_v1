Key idea:
- Abstention is architectural, not post-hoc.
- Novel inputs recruit new CCCs immediately.
- Familiar resonant inputs refine existing CCCs slowly.

Importance signal:
    importance = normalized fire frequency × confidence × recency
    if importance > lock_threshold -> freeze CCC weights
